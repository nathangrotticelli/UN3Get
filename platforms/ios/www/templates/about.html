What is U Nightlife?
U Nightlife is a mobile app created by a team of college students obsessed with events. Our goal is to see how we can improve student interaction with the event information around them.
How are events collected?
Events are collected from every user's social graph, scattered sources around the university area, and "Adding an Event" directly on the mobile app.
What is "Following" someone?
How/why should I do it?
In your profile dropdown, you have the ability to follow or unfollow your Facebook friends who use U Nightlife. When you follow someone, they get a notification, and you see when they discover and "Watch" interesting events in the future. This aids in making and spreading fun plans with the friends around you.
What is "Watching" an event?
How do I do it?
Whether you are viewing an expanded event, the List view, or Swipe view, there will be an eye icon somewhere on the page. This icon allows you to "Watch" an event, creating a clickable notification in both your profile dropdown and the profile dropdown of friends that follow you.
How do I get new events?
Logging in and pulling the event feed downwards both update your event list.
Who has access to the app?
Right now, access to the app is restriced to college students, who are verified upon entry via their social graph (Facebook) or .edu email.
How can I spread my own events?
Using our straightforward "Add an Event" feature, you can submit an event, meeting or activity for review. The UN team will contact you for further event verification.
Can I add an event to my iCal?
Yes. On an events expanded view (reached by tapping on an event in the List or Swipe view), tap the calendar icon/event date (usually the second item down on the list), and if calendar access is allowed to U Nightlife, you'll recieve a pop up confirmation, and be reminded of the event an hour prior via your iCalendar.
Where can I give feedback?
Use the "Email Us" button located on the "Help/Contact Us" section of the app (you can navigate there by tapping the menu in the right hand corner). We definitely want to hear from you, good or bad, so don't hesitate.
